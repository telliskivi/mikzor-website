<span class="red">fotograafia</span> on hea silma arendaja ning aitab
ümbruskonda analüütilisemalt jälgida, mistõttu on ta ka hea tööriist
arhitektuuri uurimisel ja <span class="blue">loomisel</span>.

with <span class="red">photography</span> one is able to study urban
space more analytically, which makes photography an important tool for
analysing and therefore creating architecture.

<span class="red">pildistan</span> peamiselt analoogkaameratega ning mul
on kogemust pimikus töötamisega.

i mainly use analog cameras for taking photos and i have experience in
working in <span class="blue">darkrooms</span>.
