a first pass at a small timber pavilion for kalamaja. single span, low
pitched roof, glulam frame in a 3.6 m bay.

the brief asked for "as close to nothing as possible" — a roof over a
patch of grass, somewhere to sit out of the rain. the answer is a
glulam portal frame on a low concrete plinth, clad on three sides with
vertical pine battens left to silver.

## studies

- model at 1:50 in chipboard and stained pine
- two structural variants: 6 m clear span, 9 m clear span
- sketch site plan with the existing oak retained

next round: detail at the eave + a 1:20 sketch of the corner.
