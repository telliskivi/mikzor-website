a short list, no scoring, no reviews. just things worth a second pour.

- **ethiopia, halo beriti, washed** — bright, jasmine, lemon peel.
  worked best as v60 at 1:16.
- **colombia, finca tamana, natural** — figs and dried apricot. tried it
  as espresso and didn't like it; the filter was good.
- **kenya, gakuyu-ini, washed** — blackcurrant, sharp acidity, won't
  forgive an under-extracted shot.
- **el salvador, los pirineos, honey** — molasses, walnut, very forgiving.
  good for cold mornings.

the grinder was a c40 on 24 clicks for filter, 11 for espresso. nothing
fancy. water was tap at ~120 ppm.
