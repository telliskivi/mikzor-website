<span class="red">coffee for me started out as a hobby in 2020</span>.
at one point i wanted to take my hobby to the next level and i was
lucky to get a job as a <span class="blue">barista at The Brick Coffee
Roastery</span> in 2021.

it was an enriching experience — involved in roasting, representing the
roastery at coffee festivals, and giving lectures about coffee.

## landmarks

- <span class="red">2021</span> — joined The Brick Coffee Roastery
- <span class="red">2023</span> — competed at the
  <span class="blue">Estonian Aeropress Championship</span>
- <span class="red">2023</span> — represented The Brick at the
  <span class="blue">Helsinki Coffee Festival</span>
- ongoing — coffee threaded into a master's thesis in architecture
