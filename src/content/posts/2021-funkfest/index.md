2021. aasta suvel korraldati Rakveres esmakordselt funkmuusika põhine
festival, <span class="red">Rakvere Funkfest</span>, kuhu paluti
disainida reklaaminstallatsioon.

in the summer of 2021 a music festival dedicated to the funk genre,
<span class="red">Rakvere Funkfest</span>, was held for the first time.
for this festival, an installation was commissioned.

## 2021 — a cassette

the first installation was inspired by music cassettes, the primary
symbol of the festival. it stood on rakvere's central square and was
later moved to <span class="blue">rakvere vallimägi</span>.

## 2022 — a disco ball

for the second year a new installation had to be designed. inspiration
came from the <span class="red">disco ball</span>, which is tightly
connected with the funk music scene. created in collaboration with
<span class="blue">Martin Sepp</span>. the disco ball itself was stolen
by local teenagers and later recovered following media attention.
