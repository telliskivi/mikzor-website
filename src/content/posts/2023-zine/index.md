<span class="red">one publication of a bi-weekly zine</span> /
2023 autumn / in collaboration with: <span class="blue">John Clayson,
Tilman Fabini, Erfan Khosravi, Mohammad Sajad Ghaderi</span>.

> "The newspaper vanishes when the wind blows. All that remains is the
> act of literation; the book."
>
> — Abbas Maroufi (1957–2022)

the project explores the liminal space between temporary and permanent
physicality. pages were printed on repurposed outdated posters from the
<span class="blue">University of Applied Arts Vienna</span>. each copy
features pages in a different order to flatten content hierarchy. a
central mind-map spread appears in every edition, presenting dualistic
frameworks for examining concepts as either temporary or permanent.
