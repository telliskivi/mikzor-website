24 pages, a5, two-colour riso on off-white stock. an edition of 80.

the first issue is mostly photography from a long week of walking, with
two short pieces of writing in the middle and a centerfold spread of
hand-set type.

## contents

1. cover (single black mark)
2. walking — fourteen photographs
3. notes from the road — short essay
4. type spread — centrefold
5. an interview — three pages
6. colophon
